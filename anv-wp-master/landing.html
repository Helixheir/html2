EMJAC aims to be the leader in the "Waste to Green Energy" industry to recycle waste tyres globally by deploying green technology with the latest blockchain technology. EMJAC uses Digital Ledger Technology (DLT), a distributed ledger made up of unchangeable, digitally recorded data in packages called blocks. These blocks of data are stored in a linear chain. Each block in the chain is cryptographically encoded with information from the block preceding it, ensuring that all data in the chain is immutable and remains unchanged. EMJAC helps in driving Environmental Change through Waste to Energy Initiatives via: Leverage the latest Blockchain technology to solve the global waste tyre stockpile
